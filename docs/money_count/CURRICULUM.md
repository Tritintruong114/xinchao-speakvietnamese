# Money Count: Survival Curriculum

**Survival Goal**: Master the "khổng lồ" (huge) numbers of Vietnamese currency and navigate your first exchange at the airport without being overwhelmed.

---

## 1. Scene Setting (Context)
You've just landed at a Vietnamese airport (SGN or HAN). You walk to a "Currency Exchange" counter. You hand over a $100 USD bill, and the clerk hands you back a thick stack of colorful notes with many zeros. You need to verify the amount and ask for smaller change for your taxi/Grab ride.

---

## 2. Visual Recognition & Safety (Visual Verification)
Viet Nam does not use coins. All transactions are in paper or **polymer** notes.

| Denomination | Material | Key Visual |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **500.000đ** | Polymer | Cyan/Blue (Largest) |
| **200.000đ** | Polymer | Red/Brown |
| **100.000đ** | Polymer | Green |
| **50.000đ** | Polymer | Pink/Purple |
| **20.000đ** | Polymer | **CAUTION**: Cyan/Blue (Looks like 500k) |
| **10.000đ** | Polymer | Yellow/Brown |

> [!WARNING]
> **The 20k vs 500k Trap**: Both notes are blue. Always count the zeros! 500.000 has 5 zeros; 20.000 has 4.

---

## 3. Core Counting System

### The 1-10 Foundation
- `1` Một, `2` Hai, `3` Ba, `4` Bốn, `5` Năm, `6` Sáu, `7` Bảy, `8` Tám, `9` Chín, `10` Mười.

### The Big Units
- **Trăm** (100)
- **Ngàn/Nghìn** (1.000)
- **Triệu** (1.000.000)
- **Tỷ** (1.000.000.000)

### Expansion Examples
- **50.000đ**: Năm mươi ngàn.
- **500.000đ**: Năm trăm ngàn.
- **5.000.000đ**: Năm triệu.

---

## 4. Real-life "Hacks" (Shortcuts)

### A. Dropping Units
Clerks talk fast. They often drop the word "Đồng" or even "Ngàn".
- Receipt says `2.000.000` -> They say: **"Hai triệu"**.

### B. The "Chục" Rule (Tens)
Used for rounded numbers.
- **50.000đ** -> **"Năm chục"** (Five tens).

### C. The "Rưỡi" Rule (The Half)
Crucial for currency exchange rates.
- **2.500.000đ** -> **"Hai triệu rưỡi"** (Two million and a half).
- **150.000đ** -> **"Một trăm rưỡi"**.

---

## 5. Mental Conversion (VND to USD)
Don't panic about being a "millionaire."

**The Golden Rule**: 
1. Drop the last **3 zeros**.
2. Divide the remainder by **25** (approximate current rate).

**Example**:
Take a **500.000đ** note.
1. `500.000` -> `500`
2. `500 / 25` = **$20 USD**.

---

## 6. Airport Roleplay dialogue

**Staff**: "Chào bạn! Bạn muốn đổi bao nhiêu?" (Hello! How much do you want to exchange?)
**Tourist**: "Tôi muốn đổi 100 Đô." (I want to exchange 100 USD.)
*Hands over cash*
**Staff**: "Tỷ giá hôm nay... Của bạn là **Hai triệu rưỡi** (2.500.000đ)."
**Tourist** (Mental check): 2.500 / 25 = 100. *Correct!*
**Tourist**: "Xin đổi tiền lẻ giúp tôi." (Please give me some small change/break this for me.)
