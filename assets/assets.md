Crimson Red (#DA251D): Primary Call-to-Action (CTA) buttons, warnings, or destructive actions.
Vibrant Yellow (#FFC62F): Highlighted cards, focal points, or XP/Coin counters.
Bubblegum Pink (#FF90E8): Gamification elements, badges, or community/social features.
Mint Green (#86EFAC): Success states, "Got it" buttons, correct answer cards, or progress bars.
Lavender Purple (#C084FC): Premium/Pro features, AI Voice elements, or paywall cards.
Soft Blue (#93C5FD): Secondary content blocks, AI chat bubbles, or info boxes.
