# XinChao 1.0.0

Welcome to XinChao, your new companion for mastering Vietnamese. We have built this version to help travelers and learners navigate the country with confidence, from your first arrival to daily interactions.

## New

*   **Survival Module:** Get instant help with a built-in currency calculator and interactive learning tools designed for real-world situations.
*   **Survival Scan:** Use your camera to scan and translate text on the go, making it easier to understand your surroundings.
*   **Refreshed Onboarding:** Enjoy a modern, welcoming design that guides you through your first steps in the app.
*   **New Audio:** Practice your pronunciation with updated, high-quality audio clips.
*   **Mobile Navigation:** Move through the app more easily with our streamlined menu and interface.
*   **Dashboard Access:** Manage your learning progress and account details in one central location.

## Improvements

*   **Landing Page:** We have updated our website to better reflect the XinChao experience and added a contact page for your feedback.
*   **Sharing:** When you share a link to XinChao, your friends will now see a clear preview, including our app name, a helpful description, and a custom image.
*   **Searchability:** We have improved our website's search engine presence to ensure you can find our resources quickly.

## Fixes

*   **Security:** We have strengthened our app by ensuring sensitive configuration files are no longer tracked, keeping your data safer.
*   **Visual Polish:** We replaced placeholder icons with the official XinChao logo for a more professional look.
