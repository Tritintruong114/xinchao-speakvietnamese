# XinChao 1.1.0

This update focuses on what **you** see and use every day in the app—clear, simple, and easy to follow.

## New

- **Easier first-time setup:** The opening screens and first steps are reorganized so you are not overwhelmed right after install.
- **Learn for real situations:** Survival packs give you ready-to-use phrases and quick tips before you head out or walk into a venue.
- **Scan when you need it:** Snap a menu or a sign to get helpful context when Vietnamese text is hard to read on the spot.
- **Website refresh:** Clearer copy and visuals so new visitors can understand XinChao faster.

## When you share a XinChao link

- People see the **app name**, a **short description**, and a **preview image** (similar to what you see when sharing on social or in chat).
- **No** account details or private data appear in that preview—only public information about XinChao.

## Improvements

- Smoother performance on more devices and on slower connections.
- Cleaner layout and easier-to-read text across screens.

## Fixes

- Corrected several display issues and awkward tap targets.

Thank you for using XinChao—if something still feels off, reach out via the contact options on the website.
