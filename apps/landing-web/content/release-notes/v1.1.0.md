# XinChao 1.0.0

Welcome to XinChao! We are excited to help you navigate Vietnam with confidence, whether you are mastering the language or exploring the country as a traveler.

## New

*   **Survival Module:** Get around easily with our new toolkit, featuring a built-in currency calculator and interactive learning assets to help you manage daily transactions and communication.
*   **Survival Scan:** Use your camera to scan and understand text on the go, making it easier to navigate signs, menus, and documents.
*   **New Audio:** Practice your pronunciation with updated, high-quality audio clips.
*   **Modern Onboarding:** Our new design makes getting started with your language journey faster and more intuitive.
*   **Improved Navigation:** We have streamlined the mobile app layout to help you find your essential phrases and tools more quickly.

## Improvements

*   **Better Sharing:** When you share a link to XinChao with friends, they will now see a clear preview of the app, including a description and a custom image to help them get started.
*   **Clearer Information:** We have updated our website copy to better reflect the current app experience, ensuring you know exactly what to expect before you download.
*   **Reliable Performance:** We have refined the mobile network experience to ensure the app remains responsive while you are on the move.

## Fixes

*   **Enhanced Security:** We have updated our internal processes to ensure sensitive configuration files are kept private and secure.
*   **Visual Polish:** Replaced placeholder icons with the official XinChao logo for a consistent look across your browser and devices.
