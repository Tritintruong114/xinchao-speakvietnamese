# XinChao 1.1.1

Welcome to the latest version of XinChao! We have been working hard to make your journey through Vietnam smoother and more intuitive, from your first steps in the app to navigating local life.

## New

*   **Improved Onboarding:** Get started faster with our updated, easy-to-follow tourist onboarding flow.
*   **Survival Scan:** Use your camera to scan and translate text on the go with our new OCR-powered Survival Scan feature.
*   **Survival Module:** Manage your trip with ease using the new currency calculator and interactive learning tools.
*   **New Audio:** Enjoy updated, high-quality audio clips to help you master your Vietnamese pronunciation.
*   **Enhanced Landing Page:** Discover our new team page and updated contact information, along with improved search engine visibility so friends can easily find and share XinChao links.

## Improvements

*   **Better Sharing:** When you share XinChao with friends, they will now see a clear preview image and a helpful description of the app.
*   **Refined Mobile Experience:** We have updated the app navigation and mobile network handling to ensure a more reliable experience while you are on the move.
*   **Updated Branding:** You will now see the official XinChao logo across all web pages and bookmarks.

## Fixes

*   **Security Update:** We have strengthened your privacy by ensuring sensitive configuration files are no longer tracked in our system.
*   **Reliability:** Resolved various build and display issues to ensure the app and website run smoothly on your device.
