# XinChao 1.0.0

Welcome to XinChao! We are excited to help you navigate your Vietnamese language journey with a fresh set of tools designed for travelers and learners alike.

## New

*   **Survival Module:** Master essential interactions with a built-in currency calculator and interactive learning assets.
*   **Survival Scan:** Use your camera to scan and understand text on the go.
*   **Onboarding Experience:** Get started quickly with our new, intuitive tourist onboarding flow.
*   **Audio Updates:** Enjoy improved sound quality for your language practice.
*   **Dashboard Access:** Manage your learning progress through the new user dashboard.
*   **Landing Page:** Explore our updated website featuring a new team page and streamlined contact information.

## Improvements

*   **Mobile Experience:** We have refined the app navigation and updated how the app handles network connections for a smoother experience.
*   **Profile Compliance:** Your user profile now meets the latest standards for a secure and consistent experience.
*   **Sharing:** When you share a link to XinChao, your friends will see a clear preview featuring the XinChao name, a helpful description, and a custom image to help them find the app easily.

## Fixes

*   **Security:** We have updated our internal configuration to ensure your sensitive data remains private and protected.
*   **Branding:** Replaced placeholder icons with the official XinChao logo for a polished look.
