# XinChao 1.1.2

Welcome to the latest version of XinChao! We have been working hard to make your Vietnamese language journey smoother, more intuitive, and better equipped for your travels.

## New

*   **Survival Modules:** Get around with confidence using our new survival tools, including a built-in currency calculator and interactive learning assets.
*   **Survival Scan:** Use your camera to scan text on the go with our new OCR-powered Survival Scan feature.
*   **Enhanced Onboarding:** Our updated onboarding flow makes it easier than ever for new travelers to get started with the app.
*   **Dashboard Updates:** We have added new survival categories and a mobile catalog to help you organize your learning library.

## Improvements

*   **Mobile Experience:** We have hardened our scanning tools and improved the overall network experience to keep you connected.
*   **Profile Compliance:** Your account and profile settings are now more secure and compliant.
*   **Sharing:** When you share a link to XinChao, your friends will now see a clear preview image and a concise description of the app.
*   **Website Clarity:** Our landing page now features updated copy, improved SEO, and a dedicated team page to help you learn more about our mission.

## Fixes

*   **Security:** We have tightened our security protocols to ensure your sensitive configuration files remain private and protected.
*   **Branding:** Replaced placeholder icons with the official XinChao logo for a more polished look.
